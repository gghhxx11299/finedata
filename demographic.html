Population Growth (2020-2025)

Total Population

132.06M
2024 population (mid-year estimate)

Ethiopia's population rose from about 118.92 million in 2020 to ~132.06 million in 2024, an 11% increase over 5 years.

Source: World Bank/UN estimates via FRED

Annual Growth Rate

2.58%
2024 growth rate (gradually slowing)

The growth rate has slowed from 2.71% in 2020 to 2.58% in 2024, showing a modest deceleration.

Source: World Bank/UN estimates

Birth and Death Rates

Birth Rate: 31.5 per 1,000 (2024)
Death Rate: 6.8 per 1,000 (2024)
Natural Increase: 24.7 per 1,000

Fertility rate: 4.1 births per woman (2024)

Source: UN Population Division

Urban vs Rural Population

Urban Population

Age and Sex Structure

Median Age

19.1 years
2025 projected median age

Rising from 18.1 years in 2020, reflecting continued high fertility.

Ethiopia has a very young population compared to the worldwide median age of 30+ years.

Sex Ratio

1.00:1
Males per female

Population is nearly evenly split by sex (approx. 50.3% male, 49.7% female).

The slight male advantage at birth (~1.03:1) evens out in adulthood.

Literacy and Education

Adult Literacy

52%
Age 15+ literacy rate (both sexes)

Improved from ~47% in 2016 to ~52% by 2017.

Female literacy significantly lags behind male literacy.

Youth Literacy

52%
Ages 15-24 literacy rate

About 59% for young men vs. 44% for young women.

Shows significant gender disparity in education.
