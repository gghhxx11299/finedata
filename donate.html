Why Support Finedata Ethiopia?

Finedatas Ethiopia is committed to providing free access to comprehensive economic, demographic, and agricultural data for everyone. Unlike many data providers, we believe that information should be accessible to all - students, researchers, businesses, and policymakers alike.

Your donation helps us maintain and expand our services, ensuring that:

Data Remains Free

We keep all our current data and resources completely free of charge

Regular Updates

We continuously update our databases with the latest information

New Data Sources

We expand our collection to include more valuable datasets

Improved Tools

We develop better visualization and analysis tools

We believe knowledge should be accessible to all, regardless of their ability to pay.

Your support makes this possible. Donate any amount you're comfortable with - every contribution helps!

Donate via Telebirr

To support our mission, please use the Telebirr app to send your donation directly to us. You can donate any amount you wish - every contribution helps us maintain and improve our free services.

How to Donate Using Telebirr

  1. Open your Telebirr app on your phone
  2. Select "Send Money" from the main menu
  3. Enter our phone number: +251 991 168 917
  4. Enter the amount you wish to donate (any amount is appreciated)
  5. Add a note: "Finedata Donation" so we can identify your contribution
  6. Confirm the transaction details
  7. Complete the transaction using your PIN

Thank you for your support! Your donation helps us keep valuable data accessible to everyone in Ethiopia.

Our Commitment to Transparency

We believe donors deserve to know how their contributions are being used. Here's how we allocate donations:

  • 70% - Data acquisition and maintenance (securing new data sources, updating existing databases)
  • 15% - Platform development and hosting (website improvements)
  • 15% - Outreach and education (helping more people access and use our data)

We're committed to keeping our overhead costs low to ensure your donation has the maximum possible impact on making data accessible to everyone in Ethiopia.
