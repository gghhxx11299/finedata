Why Finedatas Exists

I built Finedatas because Ethiopia deserves a transparent, accessible data hub — one that combines official statistics with real-world context like weather, culture, and farmer needs.

As a young Ethiopian passionate about data, I wanted to bridge the gap between national institutions and everyday users.
