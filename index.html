I built Finedatas because no single source offered Ethiopia’s full data picture — from teff yields to urban growth to forex trends. Government reports are scattered, global sites lack local context, and researchers waste time stitching fragments together.

So I created Ethiopia’s only all-in-one hub: open, reliable, and built for real-world use — whether you’re a farmer checking rain forecasts or a policymaker analyzing GDP.

— Geabral Mulugeta, Founder
