Major Crops Production

Teff Production

5.6M tons
2021 main (Meher) season production
Grown on ~2.93 million hectares by 6.65 million smallholders
Average yield: ~1.9 t/ha

Teff occupies ~30% of Ethiopia's cereal area and ~20% of grain output.

Maize Production

10.75M tons
2021/22 production (Ethiopia's top grain)
Grown on ~2.56 million hectares
Average yield: ~4.19 t/ha

Oromia and Amhara are the leading maize-producing regions.

Agricultural Inputs and Challenges

Fertilizer Use

50-80 kg/ha
Average use on cultivated land
Government heavily subsidizes fertilizers (DAP, urea)
About half of smallholders report using any fertilizer

Mechanization

2-3 tractors
Per 10,000 ha of arable land
Most farmers use animal traction or hand tools

Improved Seeds

>50% of wheat area uses certified seed
<20% for teff uses certified seed
Informal (farm-saved) seeds remain common

Sustainability Challenges

Soil Degradation

>10 t/ha/yr
Average soil loss in highlands without terraces
~70% of Ethiopia's land area is classified as degraded

Water Scarcity

Agriculture is 95% rainfed
Only ~3% of cropland irrigated
Recurring droughts severely impact production

Crop Losses

10-15%
Post-harvest losses of grain output
Due to poor storage/transport and pest outbreaks

Source: Various studies, FAO/UN databases
