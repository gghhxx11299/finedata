Frequently Asked Questions

How often is your data updated?

Our data is updated as soon as new information becomes available from official sources. Economic indicators are typically updated monthly, demographic data quarterly, and agricultural data follows seasonal reporting cycles.

Can I request specific data not available on your website?

Yes, we accept custom data requests. Please use the contact form above to describe the specific data you need, and our team will respond with availability and potential timelines.

How can I cite data from Finedata Ethiopia?

We recommend citing both Finedata Ethiopia and the original data source. For example: "[Data Title], Finedata Ethiopia, sourced from [Original Source Name]." Please include the retrieval date as our data is periodically updated.

Do you provide data analysis services?

Yes, we offer custom data analysis services for businesses, researchers, and organizations. Contact us with your specific requirements, and we'll develop a tailored solution for your needs.

Is there a cost associated with accessing your data?

Most of our data is available free of charge. However, custom data requests, detailed reports, and specialized analysis services may involve fees. We're transparent about any costs before beginning work.
